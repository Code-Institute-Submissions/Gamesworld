MORTAL KOMBAT 11

Mortal Kombat is back, and it’s better than ever! It’s time to continue this epic saga as you delve into the cinematic storyline developed by award-winning NetherRealm Studios. Take on a role of past and present characters in a brand-new narrative in Mortal Kombat 11. This new instalment in the series introduces new multiplayer modes, iconic fatalities and an impressive roster of fighters to choose from. Including fan-favourites and new characters, you can pack a punch with one of their powerful abilities and impressive fatalities.
